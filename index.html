Orbit Wallet

A clean, minimal Ethereum wallet interface in your browser. Connect with MetaMask or another compatible wallet to get started.

  • Secure – your keys stay in your wallet extension
  • Transparent – shows your current network and balance
  • Practical – send ETH in a few clicks

Use test networks for experimentation (e.g. Sepolia). Do not send large amounts of real funds from this demo UI.
